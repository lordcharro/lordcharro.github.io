About

BMI & Weight Control is a free health application to calculate the BMI, RMR and Ideal Weight. Keep track of your weight, your body mass index (BMI) and calculate the Resting Metabolic Rate (RMR). Track your weight state and BMI on charts.

  • Beautiful Design
  • User Friendly UI
  • Track the Weight and BMI on Charts
  • Real Time BMI and RMR Values
  • Ideal Weight Estimation
  • Supports two Formats of Units, Metric (kg/cm) and Imperial (lbs/in)
  • Desire Weight
  • In English and Portuguese
  • Completely Free!
